Strava has a few more steps to setup, but ultimately will be simpler as it tracks your fitness activities for you. These first few steps require using your phone.
Download Strava On your phone, start by downloading Strava from the App/Android store.
Create an Account Create an account on Strava using your email or Facebook.
 Track your activities on Strava The next time you go for a run, walk or bike ride, use Strava on your phone. Each time you do a fitness activity, you must follow these steps.
Begin recording Before your fitness activitiy, press the record button.
Set activity type Then, pick the type of activity you want to do by pressing the middle button.

A popup will come up as follows.

Connect your account to Tucan In the meantime, connect your Strava account to Tucan by pressing this button and authorizing Tucan to access your account : Add your Strava activities on Tucan After you log activities on Strava, they will show up on Tucan.
Navigate to the "add activity" menu on the map page After your fitness activity, press the "Add activity" icon on the left navigational bar.
See your Strava activities Your eligible Strava activity will show up in the list of activities. If your activitiy is not there, wait an hour before checking again. Sometimes it can take a while to get from Strava.
Add your activity Click the activity you would like to apply, and it will turn blue.

Add the activity to your progress by pressing the button at the bottom. Your pin then will move on the map.
Note: don't like importing your activities each time? Go to the settings tab, and toggle "Automatic Activity Import" so that all future actvities will be directly applied from Strava.
