Frequently Asked Questions

Using Tucan Fitness

1. Do I actually run the route on the Map?

Click to see answer.

Most of our races do NOT require you running the route on the map. Assume you are running in your own neighborhood unless your race director specifies that it's a "hybrid" race, which means that you will run the routes, but on your own time.

2. It's race day, where do I go?

Click to see answer.

Navigate to your profile, and under "Current Races" click "view map".

3. How do I use and connect with Strava?

Click to see answer.

  1. If you don't have Strava, download Strava from the app store and setup a Strava account on your phone (don't worry, it's free).
  2. After creating your Strava account, go back to our site, Tucan Fitness. Go to your profile and click "Authenticate with Strava" and follow the steps to attach your account.
  3. This will pull in any Strava activities you do, and you can access them by clicking "Log activities" on the map page.

If you are confused about how to record activities with Strava, read more on the Strava site.

4. How do I manually add my miles?

Click to see answer.

Click "Log activities" on the map page, and enter your activity information!

Click to see answer.

Strava sometimes takes a while to send activities to us, especially biking activities. If this persists for over 24 hours, go to your profile, unauthenticate from Strava, and reauthenticate with Strava. If issues persist, please email us at admin@tucan.fitness.

6. Help, I entered the wrong amount of miles!

Click to see answer.

Occasionally, this may happen. As of right now, we do not support users removing their own miles.
Please email us at admin@tucan.fitness with the amount of miles you added and the amount of miles you meant to add. We'll fix it for you.

7. What should I do if I have a payment issue?

Click to see answer.

Contact us directly at admin@tucan.fitness. We can refund you via Paypal if needed.
If you dispute the claim with Paypal, it deducts $20 from the fundraiser or race you are associated with, and no one wants that.

About Tucan Fitness Business

1. Why Tucan Fitness?

Click to see answer.

At Tucan, we are passionate about fitness and bringing people together. We believe fundraising and racing (fund-racing) should not be limited to the physical world.
Our virtual platform is inclusive of all fitness levels and fits right into your busy schedule. Ask us about how to set up your race or fundraiser today.

2. How much are your services?

Click to see answer.

It varies on the race duration and amount of participants, but it will generally cost between 5-10$/person.
For instance, we had a race with 500 people for 1 month, and each user paid $7 to the platform, and the rest went to the organization. Not too shabby! Contact us for a quote.

3. What do I need to setup a race?

Click to see answer.

Talk with us and we'll let you know more! If you decide to do a race with us, we need you to:

  • Determine the distance, dates, and timing of the race
  • Customize the route, personalize it for your organization or community
  • Set entry price and donations
  • Decide whether people compete as individuals or a team
  • Market the race to your community
