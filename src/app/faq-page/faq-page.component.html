Using Tucan Fitness

How do I use and connect with Strava?

If you don't have Strava, download Strava from the app store and setup a Strava account on your phone (don't worry, it's free) After creating your Strava account, go back to our site, Tucan Fitness. Go to your profile and click "Authenticate with Strava" and follow the steps to attach your account. This will pull in any Strava activities you do, and you can access them by clicking "Log activities" on the map page. If you are confused about how to record activities with Strava, read more on the Strava site.

How do I manually add my miles?

Click "Log activities" on the map page, and enter your activity information!
