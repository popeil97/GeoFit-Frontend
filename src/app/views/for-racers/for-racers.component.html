How Does Tucan
Fitness Work?

Step 1: Register to a race

View the races currently available on the “Races” page. Once you find one you like, register, and buy any merch you may be interested in.

Step 2: Set up your profile

Before race day, set up your profile. It’s recommended to connect to Strava to enable automatic import of activities. Change your profile pic and description so friends can find you!

Step 3: Run/walk/cycle

Once race day starts, you complete fitness activities, and record it using Strava or another device.

Step 4: Upload, interact, and repeat

After completing an activity, go back to Tucan Fitness and share your progress by uploading your activity and any pictures or statuses you want to share with the community!

Step 5: Finish!

When the race is over, you can feel good about your accomplishments and the cause you ran for! Now it’s time to think about your next race!

Interact and collaborate with other racers around the world

Participating in a virtual race allows you to:

  • Compete and share your story with other racers
  • Exercise at your own pace while providing for a good cause
  • Track your progress and statistics over the duration of the race
