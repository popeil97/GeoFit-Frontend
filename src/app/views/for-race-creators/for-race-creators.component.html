How does Tucan Fitness work?

STEP 1: CREATE A RACE

Log into your Tucan Fitness account and get started creating your race with our Creator Tool.

Step 2: Fill out the details

Add the important details including the race route, participants, fundraising merch, and other key aspects of your race.

Step 3: Share your race

Send out invitations to your race participants and share your new race on social media.

Step 4: Track your race

Keep track of your participants, settings, status of the race, and more via your Race Dashboard on your account.

Step 5: Finish!

When the race is over, collect your registration fees and send a thank you to all of your participants.
