What is Tucan Fitness?

Tucan Fitness brings community to virtual racing. We are a social fitness platform for organizations to engage their community virtually, promote healthy living, and raise funds for good causes.

We are built around community and our races empower people to participate from anywhere, anytime, and at any pace. Connect with your community and be social from a distance all while staying fit.
