We bring community to virtual racing

Tucan fitness is a social fitness platform for race and fundraiser organizers to engage their community virtually from anywhere in the world.

Get fit with your community

Tucan Fitness is built around community, with the goal that you can participate and engage with others from anywhere in the world

Share your progress

Tucan Fitness is a social platform that allows you to share pictures, videos, and your hard work with others

Bring out your competitive side

Tucan Fitness helps you achieve your personal goals, whether it's to be healthy or climb the leaderboard.

Give back to your org

Tucan Fitness allows you to raise money for a cause you care about and support your community

Our Value

For Racers

Fit fitness into your own schedule. Do not let your busy schedule stop you from climbing the leaderboard or raising funds for a good cause. Connect with other racers in your community and be social from a distance.

For Race Directors

Replace canceled races or create new events. Engage your community virtually, all without the costs required to put on in-person events. Take your event 100% virtual or create hybrid events to take racing to the next level.

For Fundraisers

A turn-key fundraising solution. Quickly set up a race, share with your community and get people moving together, all for a good cause. Choose your own custom route and engage your sponsors to make a personalized fundraiser.
